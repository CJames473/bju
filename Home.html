Leah Grace Najimy

Leah attended Berkshire Community College amid all the chaos of COVID-19, while studying classical voice with Marjorie Dix at the Berkshire Music School. She also was part of the BCC Jazz ensemble where she learned to sing more contemporary styles. In 2021 she transferred to Bob Jones University where she is pursuing a Bachelor of Music in Voice Performance. At Bob Jones, she studies opera and art song and sings with the BJU Chorale and BJU Chamber Singers. She is currently planning to finish her education at Bob Jones in December of 2024 and see where God leads her next.
